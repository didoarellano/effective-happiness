Why Code?

  • Computer technology touches every aspect of our lives and will continue to gain a greater presence in the future. It is important we not only know how to use/consume this technology but that we know how to create with this technology to make a difference.
  • Learning to program empowers children by giving them confidence as they learn to communicate and command computers.
  • Programming teaches computational, critical thinking and logic skills.
  • Children begin to see that it is possible to become digital creators instead of simply being digital consumers, thereby, allowing them to contribute, make a difference and express their creativity through technology in their chosen areas of interest.

Programs Offered

Exploring the world of Programming and Computer Science through fun and engaging programs.

Minecraft Modding

Minecraft Modding introduces programming skills through the popular Minecraft game. Kids can:

  • Create their own texture packs
  • Code "The Force" and become a Jedi Master or Iron Man
  • Create their own magic wands and sidekicks with mobs & animals
  • Create their own mini-games like Hide-and-Seek, Cave-in, Parkour, Electric Floor & Gravity

Game Programming with Scratch

Children will begin their exploration into computer programming by learning to program with blocks. Projects include:

  • Story-telling projects
  • Animation projects

Web Design Level II

We are offering a Level II Summer class to further advance students' web design and programming skills. Open to KSM students grades 7 to 12 who completed Web Design.

This class will cover the following three advanced topics in Web Design:

  • CSS Transitions & Animations
  • Advanced web layout using grids and front-end frameworks
  • JavaScript programming for interactive websites
